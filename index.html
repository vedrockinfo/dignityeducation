mission & vision

DEVI Sansthan (Dignity Educaon Vision Internaonal) is a non-profit organisaon founded in 1992 and based in Lucknow, the capital of Uar Pradesh, India’s most populous state. Collaborang with governments, NGOs, schools, and corporaons worldwide, DEVI Sansthan is accelerang FLN for children and adults.

Our Mission

To provide quality educaon for underprivileged communies by creang scalable, swi and effecve pedagogies; and implemenng them across broad geographies through partnerships with governments, schools & colleges, corporates and other NGOs.

Our Vision

A world in which every child gets a quality educaon, that goes beyond basic literacy to address key 21st century challenges, including climate change. A world in which every adult has the dignity of being able to read and write.
